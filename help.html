How do I join a Meetup group? +

Click on any group you're interested in, then click the "Join" button. Some groups may require you to answer a few questions or pay a membership fee.

How do I create my own group? +

Sign up for an account, then click "Start a Group" in the navigation. Fill in the details about your group and submit for review.

What is Meetup Pro? +

Meetup Pro is a premium subscription that gives organizers advanced tools, analytics, and priority support. Learn more about pricing.

How do I reset my password? +

Go to the login page and click "Forgot Password". Enter your email and we'll send you instructions to reset it.

Can I get a refund? +

Yes, you can request a refund within 14 days of your purchase. Contact our support team for assistance.
