Velipoja

Velipoja is a town in the municipality of Velipoja is one of Albania's most beloved
coastal gems, situated in the northernmost part of the country
near the border with Montenegro. Known for its expansive sandy beach
that stretches for over 10 kilometers, it is a favorite destination
for families and travelers seeking a mix of relaxation and natural beauty.
